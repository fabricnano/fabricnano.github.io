About FabricNano

FabricNano (fabricnano.com) is a venture-backed startup. We aim to revolutionise the synthesis of biomolecules - bioplastics, biofuels, fragrances, flavours, medicines, and more. We use nanotechnology to construct multi-enzyme cascades with increased yield, higher purity, and lower synthesis costs. You would join a passionate team at an exciting early stage to help build our culture and initial products.


Role

You will be part of a collaborative team developing in vitro metabolic systems. In particular, you will focus on the conjugation of DNA and proteins.


Responsibilities

  • You will independently analyse data, troubleshoot methods, and report outcomes to the team and in peer-reviewed publications.
  • You will collaborate with biophysicists, chemists, enzymologists and computational scientists in our team and partner organisations.
  • You will take advantage of opportunities to develop conjugation chemistries and streamline production of DNA-protein conjugates.
  • You will contribute to a passionate, creative and open-minded work environment.


Requirements

    Essential:

  • Extensive hands-on experience with chemical modification of DNA and/or protein, acquired in a PhD, post-doc, or equivalent level in industry.
  • Flexible and focused team player.

    • Desirable:

  • Experience with cloning, protein purification and handling.
  • Experience with enzyme assays.
  • Experience with DNA nanotechnology.
  • Industry experience.

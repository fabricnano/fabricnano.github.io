Meet our amazing team (formed on Cohort LD11 Entrepreneuer First!)

Grant Aarons

Chief Executive Officer


Trained in Mechanical and Chemical Engineering at the Cooper Union, Grant Aarons then worked on the Research floor at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms to aid macroeconomic policy predictions. Previously pursuing a PhD in Economics at London Business School, Grant has led fullterm course lectures in microeconomics and market/auction theory to business students and econometrics to PhD candidates.

Ferdinando Randisi

Chief Technology Officer


Studying DNA based theoretical biophysics at Oxford University and completing his PhD in Fall 2018, Ferdinando (Ferdy) is one of only a handful of computational biophysicists to have designed a coarse-grain dynamic simulations of DNA structures from first principles. Ferdy is among a few primary authors on the open-source oxDNA codebase recognized throughout the biophysics community.
